Noble.Kickz Foundation

Turning motivation into motion — rewarding academic achievement and community service with brand-new shoes.

Why We Exist

Shoes shape confidence. For many under-privileged youth, a fresh pair of shoes can change how they walk into school, how they feel about themselves, and how they show up in the world.

What We Do

We provide clean, brand-new shoes to youth who demonstrate:

  • Academic effort
  • Community service
  • Positive character

Our goal: boost confidence, reduce bullying, and encourage students to take pride in their achievements.

Who Can Apply

Students, parents, or guardians may submit an application. You will provide school information, grades, and examples of community service.
